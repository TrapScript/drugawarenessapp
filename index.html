    • Understanding Substance Addiction

    Different types of substances affect the human body differently, it is important that one identifies the effects of a particular substance on their body so as to be able to seek help that is neeeded.

    Effects of bhang abuse

    • Anxiety
    • Impaired cognitive function
    • Respiratory issues
    • Impact on mental health

    Effects of alcohol abuse

    • Addiction and dependency
    • Liver damage
    • Impaired cognitive function
    • Mental health issues
    • Social and behavioral problems

    Effects of cocaine abuse

    • Addiction and dependency
    • Cardiovascular problems
    • Respiratory issues
    • Mental health disorders
    • Social and personal problems
    • Increased risk taking behavior

    Effects of heroin abuse

    • Addiction and dependency
    • Physical health issues
    • Cognitive impairement
    • Withdrawal symptoms
    • Social and personal problems
    • Overdose
    • Infectious disease

    Support for Recovery

    After one indentifies the effects a certain substance has on them, they are able to consult a specialist who will then offer assistance based on the type of substance abused and how to recover.
